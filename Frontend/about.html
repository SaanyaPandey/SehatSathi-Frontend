About SehatSathi

SehatSathi is a social-impact health-tech platform designed to make quality healthcare accessible and approachable, especially for people in rural or underserved regions. We simplify access to verified doctors, emergency help, and local health services — all through one easy-to-use portal.



Our Mission

To ensure that every citizen — regardless of location, income, or digital literacy — can reach verified medical help, understand first aid, and access essential healthcare services without delay.



Why We Exist??

In India, thousands of lives are lost every day due to lack of awareness, delayed response in emergencies, and inaccessibility to doctors or medicines. SehatSathi is built to bridge this gap — using simple tech, human empathy, and verified networks.



What we Offer??

a. Contact a Doctor


View and reach verified doctors who are available for basic guidance or future appointments.



b. First Aid Help


Access step-by-step emergency guidance (in visual + text format) for common injuries and conditions.



c. Nearby Hospitals & Clinics


Discover government hospitals, clinics, and pharmacies around you — even in low-network zones.



d. Easy Availability of Medicines


Get basic over-the-counter medicines delivered to your home with local pharmacist tie-ups.



Made For Everyone!!

  • Full-privacy and no advertisements or Data-Sharing.
  • Easily Accessible to all.
